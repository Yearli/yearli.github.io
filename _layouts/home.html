About us

Yearli is an initiative to bring yearbooks like this into the hands of graduating students across India, complete with a quote and picture of each student. While the first project is specifically an yearbook for the CSE batch of 2022 at NIT Silchar, we wish to eventually create yearbooks for older and younger batches of all disciplines to remember their time at college and the people they've interacted with there.
